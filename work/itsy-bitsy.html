ITSY BITSY ZINE CLUB

A community that shares the magic of making and receiving

The Itsy Bitsy Zine Club is a community dedicated to people interested in making zines, prints, stickers, photos, or whatever our heart contents! We have a semi-monthly subscription where a zine bundle filled with works made by our members gets delivered straight to people's residential college entryways or off-campus housing mailbox. We hope to inspire people to imagine a litte bigger, share a little more, and make a little zine with us.
